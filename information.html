2.Emergency Procedures

  • Fire Emergency: In case of fire, follow evacuation routes to designated assembly points. Do not use elevators during a fire emergency.

  • Natural Disasters: For earthquakes, seek shelter under sturdy furniture. Follow designated routes during floods. Stay informed about hurricane procedures.

  • Medical Emergencies: Administer basic first aid. Locate first aid kits placed in common areas and know the locations of automated external defibrillators (AEDs).

3.Communication Plan

  • Emergency Notification System: Receive alerts through the college app, text messages, and email. Listen for sirens on campus.

  • Communication Protocols: Use designated communication channels and follow instructions from campus authorities during emergencies.

4.Important Resources

  • Campus Map: Explore the interactive map to locate emergency resources, exits, and safe zones in Bhopal.

  • Emergency Kit Checklist: Prepare a personal emergency kit with essentials like water, flashlight, and first aid supplies.
